The New Easy Talk Widget for Android

We are excited to announce our Easy Talk widget for all Voxer users on Android devices! You can now listen and respond to chats directly from the home screen.

Navigating dozens of apps can be frustrating. Using the widget you can access your most important Voxer chats with a simple swipe.

With the Voxer Easy Talk widget you can:

  • Assign one chat as ‘top priority’ to always appear first in the widget

  • Easily navigate between your chosen chats with the arrows

  • Enjoy the convenience of sending voice, text and images from your home screen!

In addition to the widget, this version includes a number of bug fixes and improvements. Upgrade from Google Play, or for more information about how it works visit our support page.

As always, if you have any questions or feedback, please email support@voxer.com.
